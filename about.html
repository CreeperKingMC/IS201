About Me

Who am I?

My name is Gabriel Antonio Jimenez. I am from Austin, TX, and I currently study electrical engineering here at BYU Provo. I am loving my IS 201 class and it has been interesting learning how to create and edit a website!

I have been at BYU for 2 years now, and the previous 2 years I was serving my mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I served the first 17 months in Nebraska and my last 7 months in Santiago, Chile.

I come from a family of 6! My dad was born in Mexico and my mom was born in New Zealand. I have a younger sister who I'm currently living with and 2 younger brothers back home with my family. We love to go out to eat together and watch movies, especially Star Wars and anime!
